About Eco Pulse

Eco Pulse is a comprehensive net zero campus simulation platform designed to help educational institutions monitor, understand, and optimize their environmental impact. This web-based system combines real-time data visualization with interactive student engagement tools.

Our Mission

To empower students and faculty with data-driven insights into campus energy consumption, waste management, and carbon footprint, fostering a culture of sustainability and environmental responsibility.

Technology

Eco Pulse is built with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript for the frontend, with Node.js and Express powering the backend. Real-time data synchronization is handled through Socket.IO for instant metric updates across all connected users.

How It Works

For Professors: Access a read-only dashboard displaying campus-wide energy usage, solar generation, waste levels, and carbon metrics. Use the data to inform sustainability initiatives and educational curriculum.

For Students: Access your personal energy control interface to toggle chargers and lights ON/OFF. Receive rule-based notifications and earn eco-points for optimizing energy usage and making sustainable choices.

Simulation Note

Please note that all data in Eco Pulse is simulated for demonstration purposes. This is a proof-of-concept application designed to showcase how real campus data would be visualized and analyzed in a production environment.
